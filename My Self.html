Labrador Retriever

Description

    The Labrador Retriever or Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog.
It was developed in the United Kingdom from fishing dogs imported from the colony of Newfoundland (now a province of Canada) , and was named for the Labrador region of that colony. It is among the most commonly kept dogs in several countries, particularly in the Western world.
